How we work

Our programmes combine community engagement, environmental education, government collaboration and structured follow-up. We use a five-stage model to move from local evidence to practical action.

Assess

Identify local needs, stakeholders, barriers and existing resources.

Co-design

Develop activities with schools, young people, communities and government.

Implement

Deliver education, mobilisation and practical environmental action.

Monitor

Track participation, outputs, behaviour and implementation progress.

Learn & Scale

Use evidence to improve delivery, sustain partnerships and replicate what works.
