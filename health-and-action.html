Bringing Environmental Health Education to Queen Elizabeth Girls School

We are excited to announce our upcoming Environmental Health Education Program at Queen Girls Elizabeth School, Ilorin, Kwara State.

    This program will focus on six essential pillars of environmental and personal well-being:
  • WASH & Personal Hygiene: Handwashing with soap, regular bathing, and clean uniforms.
  • Sanitation & Waste Management: Proper waste disposal, waste segregation, and maintaining clean restrooms.
  • Water Safety: Access to safe, treated water and proper storage.
  • Vector Control: Eliminating mosquito breeding sites and sleeping under treated nets.
  • Clean Environment: Keeping classrooms and schools clean, and planting trees for fresh air.
  • Healthy Food & Diet: Eating nutritious food and washing fruits before eating.

This is a vital initiative to empower our students and community with the knowledge to make healthier choices and live better lives. Lets build a healthier community, together.
