Our story

Grace Life Foundation for Social Impacts is a registered Nigerian non-governmental organisation working at the intersection of environmental health, public health, youth leadership and community development.

About Us

About the Foundation

Grace Life Foundation for Social Impacts advances environmental sustainability, public health and youth-led development through education, community action and strategic partnerships. The Foundation responds to everyday environmental challenges that affect children, young people and vulnerable communities, including poor sanitation and hygiene, unmanaged waste, environmental degradation and limited access to practical environmental education.
The Foundation works with government institutions, public schools, community stakeholders and volunteers to create healthier learning environments, strengthen environmental responsibility and support young people to take practical action in their schools and communities.

Our Mission

Healthier, safer and more resilient communities where every child and young person can thrive in a clean and sustainable environment.

Our Vision

To improve environmental health and community resilience through education, WASH action, youth leadership, advocacy and strategic partnerships.

Impact Focus

Our work is centered on

Reducing environmental health risks

Promoting safer community practices

Improving conditions in public and shared spaces

Supporting long-term behavioural change
